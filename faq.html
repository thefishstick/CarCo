Frequently Asked Questions

We're happy to answer any questions you have. The most common question we hear are answered below.

About Product

What are driveway bollards, and how do they prevent auto theft?
Driveway bollards are retractable barriers installed in driveways to prevent unauthorized vehicle access. They deter theft by physically blocking entry.
Are the bollards suitable for use in winter conditions?
Yes, our bollards are designed to withstand harsh Canadian winters. They are built to operate smoothly in cold temperatures and snow.
Are there any safety features to prevent accidents or damage to vehicles?
Yes, our bollards are equipped with safety sensors to prevent accidental activation and damage to vehicles.
Are your bollards environmentally friendly?
Our bollards are designed with energy efficiency in mind, and we strive to minimize their environmental impact.

About Installation

Where does your service operate?
We proudly serve the greater Toronto area, providing our driveway bollard installation service to enhance security for local residents.
How long does the installation process take?
Installation typically takes a few hours, but it may vary based on the specific requirements of your driveway.
Do I need to be present during installation?
It's not necessary for you to be present during installation, as our professional technicians can complete the setup efficiently.
What happens if there's a power outage?
Our bollards have a backup power source to ensure continued security during power outages.

About Pricing

How much does installation cost?

Our subscription fee is $50 per month, which includes free maintenance.

About Other

Do you offer maintenance and repairs?
Yes, we provide free maintenance and repairs as part of your subscription to ensure your bollards remain in excellent condition.
Do you offer 24/7 customer support?
Yes, we provide 24/7 customer support to address any questions or concerns you may have.
How often is maintenance required?
Maintenance is typically never required, but it can be adjusted based on your usage and needs.
What is the warranty on your bollards?
Our bollards come with a standard warranty, and we offer extended warranty options for added peace of mind.
